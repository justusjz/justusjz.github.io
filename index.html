    Creating a Blog

    February 20, 2024

    I'm always working on lots of projects privately, and I enjoy telling my coworkers about it. A couple of weeks ago, one of them proposed that I should write a blog about those projects, so other people could see what I'm doing and maybe even give me some ideas. After thinking about it for a while, I decided that it was a great idea, and got to work. At first, I researched some systems to design blogs. One of the first ones I found was Jekyll, which can be easily used with GitHub Pages. However, Jekyll doesn't have direct support for comments, and I thought a comment system would be quite nice, because I like to know what others are thinking about my work. Also, sometimes I overlook something very simple, so it might be good to have someone point those things out. Therefore, I decided to abandon Jekyll, and find my own solution.
